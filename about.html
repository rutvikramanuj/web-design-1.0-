About ADVISS

Introduction

ADVISS initiative (advanced digital visual inspection and supervision system) is the computer vision based project to make students management system more smarter than earlier boring and non accurate systems. Also we are trying to create modules that help parents to zoom on student, for whom the parents are looking for.

The mentors and examiners will also able to track the student behaviour while lectures and exams and also able to take the photo capture of any suspicious activity of student for their improvement purposes.

To

Manage student efficiently

Improve examination supervision

Inspect every suspicious activities

Achieve maximum security of students data

Create the stack of AI and ML based techniques to improve the student management system
